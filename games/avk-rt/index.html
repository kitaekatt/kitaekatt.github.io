About

Built in C with Flecs ECS, it simulates archers (red) fighting knights (blue) on a 16:9 grid with sprite animations. Combat is turn-based -- all units move one tile per turn. Knights have range 1 and 25 HP. Archers have range 3 and 10 HP. All attacks deal 5 damage.

Knights need to surround archers to kill them -- with equal movement speed and shorter range, knights must corner archers against walls or each other.

Check out the minimalist version for large-scale battles.
