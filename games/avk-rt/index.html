Archers vs Knights

A turn-based auto-battler on a 16:9 grid. Red archers and blue knights fight until one side is eliminated. All units move one tile per turn. Built in C with Flecs ECS.

How It Works

Each turn: all units move, then all units attack. Movement and attacking is grid based (up, down, left, right).

Knights charge toward the nearest archer. When two options are equally good, they try to make room for allies, and corner their enemies against the walls.

Archers try to stay at exactly their attack range. Too close and they retreat; too far and they advance. Same tiebreakers as knights.

Attack: each unit hits the lowest-HP enemy within range. Knights must be adjacent; archers can shoot up to 3 tiles away.

Win condition: the game ends when one side has no units left.
